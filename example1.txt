currentChangeRulebase

Rule 1 If temperature is high or current is high then change_in_current will be reduce
Rule 2 If temperature is low and current is high then change_in_current will be no_change
Rule 3 If temperature is medium then change_in_current will be no_change
Rule 4 If temperature is low and current is low then change_in_current will be increase
